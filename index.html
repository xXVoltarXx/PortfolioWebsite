About Me

Hello! My name is Michael Kleinbriel. I am an aspiring game developer with an emphasis on VR.
I have experience with programming in Python, HTML, JavaScript, Java, C# and C++. Most of my programming experience is in a game development context within Unity and Unreal Engine.
I have been a player and level designer on multiple team projects and I've been a solo indie developer on a couple of my own projects.
I have also been using Blender for a little over 7 years in order to make 3D Models and multiple short films.
